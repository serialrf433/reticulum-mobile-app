• You can now switch off any transport you don't use — Bluetooth LE, Bluetooth Classic, TCP, or USB — under Settings → Connection → Transports. The app only runs the connections you actually rely on.
• New (experimental) USB serial support: connect a wired RNode over a USB-OTG cable, no Bluetooth needed. Off by default — turn it on under Transports.
• Under-the-hood security hardening: dropped an unused dependency and locked in the radio-frame size limits with new tests.
