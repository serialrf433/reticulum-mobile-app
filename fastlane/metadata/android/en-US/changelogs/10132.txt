• Fix message-ordering bug. The LXMF wire timestamp was set when the
  send finished packing (after link establishment / retries), not
  when the user tapped Send. If two messages were composed in order
  but the later one had a faster link than the earlier one, the
  recipient's conversation view could place them out of order.
  Now uses the tap-Send time saved on the local row, so what the
  recipient sees matches the order Send was tapped — including for
  pictures, where the slow Resource-send path made this most
  visible.
