• Diagnostics log: routine TCP disconnects (NAT timeout, Doze mode,
  network change) no longer surface as "TCP: read loop crashed" —
  the log line now reads "TCP: read loop ended ... — supervisor
  will reconnect" so it doesn't look like an app crash. Unexpected
  socket failures still log as "crashed" since they're actually
  worth investigating.
• Settings → Connection section now offers a one-tap "Disable
  battery optimization" button when the app isn't on the OS's
  exempt list. Without the exemption, Samsung / Xiaomi / OnePlus
  battery managers will kill the foreground service after a few
  minutes of screen-off and inbound messages stop landing until
  the user reopens the app. Tapping the button opens the system
  dialog; the app works either way, just with less aggressive
  reconnect churn if accepted.
