• Security hardening across the wire-facing code, from a full audit:
  malformed packets that could crash the app or exhaust memory are now
  rejected cleanly, messages with a forged signature are dropped
  instead of shown, and replayed inbound messages are deduplicated
  durably.
• Reticulum Relay Chat: rooms you have joined are automatically
  re-joined when the app reconnects to a hub — no more leaving and
  re-joining to get messages flowing again.
• Reticulum Relay Chat: a stale hub connection is now detected and
  shown as disconnected, instead of falsely reading "connected".
• Reticulum Relay Chat: the Nodes tab has a new RRC filter to list
  just the relay-chat hubs.
