• Auto-reconnect on launch: the app now re-establishes the BLE /
  Bluetooth / TCP transport it was last connected to when it starts,
  so you don't have to tap Connect every time. An explicit Disconnect
  is remembered — it won't reconnect after you go offline on purpose.
  Toggle it off in Settings → Connection.
• Reticulum Relay Chat: hub sessions you had open are re-opened
  automatically once a transport is back up, and your joined rooms
  come back with them.
